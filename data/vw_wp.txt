Volkswagen (German pronunciation: [ˈfɔlksˌvaːɡŋ̍] - About this sound listen
(help·info)), shortened to VW, is a German automaker founded on May 28, 1937 by the
German Labour Front and headquartered in Wolfsburg. It is the flagship marque of the
Volkswagen Group, the largest automaker by worldwide sales in 2016.[1]

Volkswagen is German for "people's car", and the company's current international
advertising slogan is just "Volkswagen".[2][3] American English pronunciation is
approximately "volks wagon" (About this sound listen (help·info))
